This project dealt with data containing index prices. Each row in the file contains a daily record of the price of the S&P500 Index from 1950 to 2015.

The columns of the dataset are:

    Date -- The date of the record.
    Open -- The opening price of the day (when trading starts).
    High -- The highest trade price during the day.
    Low -- The lowest trade price during the day.
    Close -- The closing price for the day (when trading is finished).
    Volume -- The number of shares traded.
    Adj Close -- The daily closing price, adjusted retroactively to include any corporate actions. Read more here.
